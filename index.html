What We Do

Full-Spectrum Tower Services

From the ground up to the antenna tip — we handle every phase of your wireless infrastructure project.

📶

Antenna & Equipment Installation

Professional installation of antennas, RRUs, and associated hardware across all major OEM platforms. We follow carrier-specific standards to the letter.

🔧

Tower Maintenance & Repair

Scheduled preventive maintenance, emergency troubleshooting, and structural repairs that keep your towers performing at peak efficiency year-round.

🏗️

Civil Construction & Build-Out

Ground-up tower construction including foundations, monopoles, guyed towers, and rooftop installations with full site acquisition support.

⚡

Power & Grounding Systems

Installation and testing of DC power plants, battery backup systems, and comprehensive tower grounding to ANSI/TIA-222 standards.

🔌

Coax & Fiber Cable Management

Expert installation, weatherproofing, and management of coaxial cables, fiber optic runs, and hybrid cable systems for optimal signal integrity.

🛡️

Safety Inspections & Audits

Comprehensive structural assessments, load analyses, and safety audits delivered with detailed reports and remediation recommendations.

Safety First

Industry-Leading Safety Standards

Every technician, every site, every time. Our safety program exceeds OSHA and ANSI requirements.

NATE Certified

Certified Tower Climbers

100% of our field technicians hold current NATE certifications with ongoing recertification requirements and documented competency assessments.

OSHA 10/30

OSHA Compliance

All crew members complete OSHA 10 training; supervisors hold OSHA 30. We maintain comprehensive safety documentation for every project.

RF Awareness

RF Safety Training

Mandatory RF awareness and non-ionizing radiation protection training. We use calibrated RF meters on every active site before any work begins.

Rescue Ready

Rescue & First Aid

Every crew carries rescue kits and includes at least one trained rescue technician. We drill rescue scenarios quarterly to maintain peak readiness.

Insured

Comprehensive Coverage

$10M general liability, workers' compensation, and professional liability insurance. Additional endorsements available per carrier requirements.

Drug-Free

Drug-Free Workplace

Pre-employment and random drug screening program. We participate in the Hair Follicle Testing Program required by all major carriers.
